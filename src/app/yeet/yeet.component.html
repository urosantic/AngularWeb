Wholesale Deal eCommerce

WHOLESALE DEALS,
DELIVERED

Objective:

Our team conducted qualitative research at Costco and BJ’s wholesale clubs and realized certain issues with the wholesale shopping experience.. People above 40 years old typically planned to use their weekend time to drive to the nearest wholesale club, look for parking, wait in long lines to pay and carry heavy things to their car. We created YEET to simplify everyone’s life with wholesale shopping delivered on-demand. The way it should be in 2019!
